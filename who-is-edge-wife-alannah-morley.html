Who is Edge wife Alannah Morley?

Canadian professional wrestler and actor Adam Joseph Copeland was born on October 30, 1973. Currently, under contract with WWE, he competes on the Raw brand with the ring name Edge. One of the all-time great professional wrestlers is thought to be him.

After making his debut in 1992, Copeland competed in singles matches and tag team matches, the latter of which he did with his longtime friend Christian Cage. Copeland wrestled in a variety of indie promotions. Copeland joined the World Wrestling Federation (WWF, now WWE) in 1997 as a developmental wrestler, and in 1998, as Edge, he made his television debut.

Who is Edge wife Alannah Morley?

Adam Joseph Copeland professionally known as “edge”, a former wrestler, initially wed Alannah Morley. Before separating, the pair had been married for a few years.

In a low-key ceremony, the pair got married in the latter part of 2001. They allegedly met for the first time in 1998 and had a romantic relationship for a few years before exchanging vows. Edge’s career in professional wrestling was also booming at the time.

Morley was born and raised in Canada’s Ontario. She grew up primarily in Ontario where she lived with her family. She also has a brother who was a former Canadian professional wrestler by the name of Val Venis. After appearing in the WWF and WWE from 1998 to 2009, her brother gained notoriety. She has a net worth of $1.5 million.
