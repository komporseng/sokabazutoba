Jennifer Flavin is an American former model as well as a current entrepreneur and business owner. She was signed by the Elite modeling agency at age eighteen after winning a modeling competition. Her career in modeling started with print work for magazines such as Glamour and Vogue. In 1992, Flavin began dating Sylvester Stallone, with whom she has three daughters: Sophia Rose (born 1996), Sistine Rose (born 1998), and Scarlet Rose (born 2002). The couple married in 1997.

Jennifer Flavin Husband

Jennifer Flavin met Sylvester Stallone in California at a Beverly Hills restaurant, in 1988. Duo in a relationship until 1994, when Stallone wrote her a letter saying that he was having an affair with model Janice Dickinson, and that he was the father of her infant daughter. After DNA tests had shown that he was not the father, the relationship ended.

In 1995, she reconciled with Stallone. Couple tied knot on May 17, 1997 in a civil ceremony at The Dorchester Hotel in London, followed by a chapel ceremony at Blenheim Palace in Oxfordshire. They have three daughters, including actress and model, Sistine Stallone. On August 19, 2022 Flavin filed for a divorce from Stallone in the state of Florida.
