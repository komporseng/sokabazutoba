First Look At Kid Flash's Costume!

Ever since the introduction of Keiynan Lonsdale as Wally West at the beginning of Season 2 of The CW’s hit show ‘The Flash’, fans have been wondering whether or not we would be seeing him suit up as Kid Flash. While there were hints that this might happen throughout season 2, it has now been confirmed and we are incredibly excited about it!

While the plot of season 3 has already been announced to the public, details about where the writers will take the show are still under wraps. All we know so far is that it will focus on the 2011 comic book issue named ‘Flashpoint’ (more details on that here). West’s introduction as Kid Flash could be directly linked to this storyline, as there is an opening for a new Speedster, with Barry Allen no longer being The Flash. Just last week it was also announced that Tom Felton will feature throughout the season as Julian Dorn and now, a first look at Wally West in his Kid Flash costume has been released to the public eye.

We are very happy with this design. While the designers have made a few changes to the original comic book costume, as always they have stayed through to the character and we can’t wait to see what Lonsdale can do in this attire! Season 3 premiers on The CW on October 4th.
