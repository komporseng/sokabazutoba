Why Rampage Jackson Can Make a Boxing Comeback in 2024

Former UFC champion Rampage Jackson is making waves with his plans for a boxing comeback in 2024. Let's delve into the reasons why he can make a successful return to the boxing ring.

Strong Desire for Rematches

Rampage Jackson's desire for rematches with former UFC opponents such as Rashad Evans and Wanderlei Silva showcases his determination to settle scores from his time in the UFC. This desire demonstrates his competitive spirit, which could drive him to succeed in the boxing arena.

Transitioning from MMA to Boxing

After almost nine years away from the UFC Octagon, Jackson is transitioning to a boxing career. His previous experience in the MMA world, including his history with renowned fighters, provides him with a solid foundation for transitioning into the boxing ring. This transition highlights his versatility and adaptability as a fighter.

High-Profile Matches

Jackson's upcoming high-profile boxing match against former heavyweight boxing champion Shannon Briggs demonstrates that he is not shying away from challenging opponents. This willingness to take on formidable opponents suggests that he is confident in his abilities, positioning him for a successful boxing comeback.

Previous Boxing Experience

Having already stepped into the boxing ring once in April 2023, where he emerged victorious in a two-on-two boxing match, Jackson has demonstrated his capability in the boxing arena. This prior experience could serve as a valuable asset in his boxing comeback, providing him with the necessary foundation to excel in future matches.

Resilience and Determination

Despite experiencing setbacks in his MMA career, including a knockout loss to Fedor Emelianenko in late 2019, Jackson's resilience and determination to pursue a boxing career are evident. His ability to bounce back from defeats and channel his energy into a new endeavor showcases his mental fortitude, which could play a pivotal role in his boxing comeback.

Strategic Matchmaking

Jackson's strategic approach to matchmaking, particularly his desire to fight opponents with less boxing experience after his high-profile match against Shannon Briggs, reflects a calculated and focused mindset. This strategic approach indicates that he is carefully planning his boxing comeback, positioning himself for success in future matchups.

Physical Preparedness

Jackson's commitment to getting in fighting shape for his comeback, as evidenced by his acknowledgment of the need for physical transformation, underscores his dedication to the sport. His focus on physical preparedness indicates that he is taking the necessary steps to ensure that he is in optimal condition for his boxing comeback.

Promotional Ambitions

Expressing a desire to promote the UFL and pursue a warm-up fight in the UFL before venturing into boxing matches reveals Jackson's broader promotional ambitions. His interest in promoting the sport and engaging in various types of fights signifies his enthusiasm for the sport, which could contribute to a successful boxing comeback.

Rampage Jackson's strong desire for rematches, transition from MMA to boxing, high-profile matches, previous boxing experience, resilience and determination, strategic matchmaking, physical preparedness, and promotional ambitions collectively position him as a compelling figure capable of making a successful boxing comeback in 2024. If he continues to channel his competitive spirit and dedication into his boxing career, he has the potential to leave a significant impact in the world of boxing.
