Who Is Mina Starsiak From Good Bones? Her Age Wikipedia &amp; Husband, Net Worth 2022

Mina Starsiak is an American actress who rose to fame with her appearance in the HGTV series, Good Bones, a reality TV show about transforming rundown houses.

Before she found her love for home renovation, Mina worked as a waitress till she bought her own home. She spent a lot of time rebuilding her own home, and that's when she realized that she loved the whole process of renovating houses.

[image-1]

With her mom, Karen, a defense attorney, she started a company named Two Chicks and A Hammer. Their company focused on neighborhood revitalization in areas of Indianapolis like Bates Hendricks and Fountain Square.

After the success of their company, HGTV spotted the duo and gave them a platform in their network with the hit show, Good Bones, where they help homeowners fix their property into their dream house.

Since the duo achieved a lot of fame and success with their TV show, Mina and Karen established another company named Two Chicks District Co and merged it with the Two Chicks with a Hammer company.
