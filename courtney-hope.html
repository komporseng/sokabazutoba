Courtney Hope – Professional Life

Courtney Hope began her career as a fitness teacher at Cardio Barre. In 2007, Hope landed her first appearances in “Born in the USA,” “Out of Jimmy’s Head,” and “Grey’s Anatomy”.

With smaller parts in shows, Hope progressed to more notable parts in “Mob Rules,” “CSI: Miami,” “Criminal Minds,” “NCIS: Los Angeles,” “NCIS, Bones,” “Quantum Break,” and “Transparent”.

Her first leading part was in “Prowl,” which was followed by “Revan,” “Swelter,” “Allegiant,” and  “Displacement”. Her biggest break came in 2017 when she landed the role of Sally Spectra on the soap opera “The Bold and the Beautiful”.

“A Merry Christmas Match” (2019) and “A Friends Obsession” (2019) are two of her other works (2018). In November 2020, she will appear as Sally Spectra in the CBS serial opera “The Young and the Restless”.
