Meet Maria Libri, SNL Alum Chris Kattan Fiancee

Maria and Chris Kattan made their relationship IG official in Feb 2022, and just a little over a year, the duo got engaged during a Wilco concert in Chicago. “Jeff Tweedy, the lead singer, stopped the concert as I kneeled and she said ‘Yes!’ Jeff said, “She said ‘yes’ everybody!” to the audience,” Chris recalled. Furthermore, Jeff also followed the proposal with one of Maria’s favorite songs “I’m the Man Who Loves You.”

“Thank you Wilco and Jeff and everyone involved in making this incredible moment actually happen. I’m forever grateful. I love you, Maria,” Chris later wrote on IG.

Talking about their relationship, Chris once shared in an interview, “Maria and I fully accept each other for who we are.” And it wasn’t just the things they had in common, but also they embraced their quirks and differences — “and we don’t love each other in spite of them, we love each other because of them,” he explained.

Chris added that it was the moment he first met her and got to time together for a few months that he realized their “creative brains were also totally in sync.”

Likewise, Maria felt the same way about Chris. “It’s funny how life works sometimes and gives you gifts when you’re not looking for them,” she said. Also, recalling their first encounter, she pointed out that they were immediately comfortable with one another. “Call it weird, mystical, or magical, it’s like we’d known each other in a past life. After spending that first day together (though I didn’t know how), I just somehow knew that my life would never be the same after meeting Chris.”
