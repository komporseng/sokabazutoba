Samantha Chapman Bio (Wiki)

Who is Samantha Chapman? 

Samantha Chapman is a British makeup artist. She was a makeup artist for the shows like the Milan and Paris fashion weeks.

She has worked with celebrities like Val Kilmer, Zac Efron, Neve Campbell, Lily Cole, etc.

Samantha Chapman: Age, Siblings, Parents, and Education

Samantha Chapman was born on 23 August 1977 in England. her nationality is British and her ethnicity is English.

Her mother’s name is Judy Chapman and her father’s name is unknown. She has two brothers, Jim Chapman and John Chapman. Both his brothers are Youtubers. Her sister’s name is Nicola Haste.

There is no information on Samantha’s educational background.

Samantha Chapman: Professional Life and Career

Samantha Chapman has been a make-up artist for more than a decade. She was part of the UK M.A.C pro team, often traveling for different shows such as the Milan and Paris Fashion Week.

She has worked with Photographers including Tony McGee and Lord Snowdon.

Her work has appeared in Harpers, Tatler, Brides, Elle, Cosmo, Company, The Sunday Times, and Sunday Telegraph.

Samantha has also worked with celebrities such as Paul McCartney, Peaches Geldof, Ashley Tisdale, Charlotte Church, Myleene Klass, Liberty Ross, Val Kilmer, Zac Efron, Neve Campbell, and Lili Cole.

Samantha Chapman is a popular British makeup artist who is mostly known for her collaboration with Paris Presents to create an inexpensive makeup brush line named Real Techniques.

In 2008, she also founded the YouTube channel Pixiwoo, which she shares with her sister Nicola. Together they present public their tutorials and reviews of many different beauty products.
