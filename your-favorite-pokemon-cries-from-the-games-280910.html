Your favorite Pokemon cries from the games...

Hey everybody, I wanted to post this because I was recently loafing around my Home Dex and for giggles I started to listen to almost every Pokemon cry there is, including the alternative forms and all (e.g. the cry while Gigantamaxing), and I remembered just how some of these electronic cries are so awesome, for different reasons. It was also very fun to remember some of the cries from older generations.

Here are some of my favorite cries:

GARDEVOIR - it sounds like it is singing, and it is a very pleasing sound.
JYNX - probably one of the funniest cries every. Another singing, but much more comedic.
KRICKETUNE - 'Nuff said. Who DOESN'T like the strange and extremely long cry of this Mon?
GROOKEY - An extremely cute cry. So fitting.
MAREEP - It sounds like it is saying "mareeep", as a sheep would if it would be able to talk. So funny.
AMAURA / AURORUS - Both cries are very majestic and unique.
DELPHOX - It sounds like it is screaming, but in a very comedic form (it could also be screaming mixed with laughing...). Also very unique cry.
SCOLIPEDE / GIGALITH - I put them together not because they sound similar (akin to Charizard and Rhyhorn), but because both sound SO COOL, like they're big dinosaurs growling.
GIGANTAMAX MEOWTH - I don't know why, but this one cracks me up every time I hear it. "Miao Miao Miaoooo". Also the fact that it sounds just like anime Meowth is amazing.
CHINGLING - Awww, this sounds just like a Christmas bell (so fitting), and I love it for this!!! I most certainly am a fan of Christmas.
HONCHKROW - While it sounds a bit like a generic bird, it does sound like he's THE boss, as he should. I can't explain this better, I just feel like he is a honcho with his cry.
GLACEON - Another extremely adorable cry for an extremely adorable Pokemon. I just love how adorable Glaceon is all around. Maybe Stufful's cry is objectively more adorable, but I chose Glaceon for how cute it is.

So, which are yours? It can be because it sounds awesome and intimidating, adorable, very funny, unique, o just because you just like the sound and find it memorable.
Thanks for reading.
