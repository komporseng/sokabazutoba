Bill Busbice: Reality TV Star, Entrepreneur, and Hunting Enthusiast

Bill Busbice has a net worth of $100 million. He is the patriarch of the family that appeared in the A&E reality television series “Country Buck$”. Busbice formerly owned a trucking company that he sold for a substantial amount of money. He also co-founded Wildgame Innovations, which specializes in products for deer hunters. Thanks to the success of Wildgame, the Busbice family was able to acquire several other hunting companies, including Barnett Crossbows and Flextone Game Calls. They also own a timber company that includes a 28,000 acre property in Northern Louisiana with a luxury hunting lodge for wealthy hunting enthusiasts.

Bill and his wife, Beth, have been married for 36 years and have two sons, Matt and Ryan, and one daughter, Sarah. Bill has also developed a mobile app for the trucking industry called HWY Pro, which aims to improve the efficiency of product transportation. Similar to Uber, HWY Pro seeks to streamline transportation needs.
