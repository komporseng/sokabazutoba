Are Francia Raisa and Selena Gomez still friends? What happened with Francia Raisa and Selena Gomez?

Selena Gomez’s career has been a heck of a rollercoaster. Despite writing some well-known songs, the pop singer is constantly making headlines due to personal life experiences. It was her relationship with Justin Bieber for some time, but things got even worse when it was revealed that she had broken off contact with her kidney donor, Francia Raisa.

Are Francia Raisa and Selena Gomez still friends?

Selena was diagnosed with lupus in 2015, as most people are privy to. Months later, the actress needed a kidney transplant, and it was her best friend Francia who made the big sacrifice.

The two stunning ladies have known each other since childhood and have remained close friends ever since. Despite their strong bond and sacrifice, Selena Gomez and Francia Raisa’s relationship suffered a setback in 2018.

According to some reports, it occurred as a result of Gomez’s unhealthy lifestyle after their successful surgery. Selena Gomez was said to have resumed her alcohol consumption.

It was absolutely fatal for her well-being, to the point where she could have lost her life. The unhealthy lifestyle allegedly started after Justin Bieber married Hailey Bieber.

What happened with Francia Raisa and Selena Gomez?

The former couple had always been in an on-and-off affair since 2010. To this day, Jelena fans believe that they are meant to be. One can only imagine how difficult the singer-actress would have found it.

Francia reached out to her celebrity friend that she was just making bad decisions and that she was worried about her. But out of anger and desperation, Gomez made a big deal out of her friend’s candid advice and decided to cut ties with her.

She did, however, thank her on World Kidney Day this year.
