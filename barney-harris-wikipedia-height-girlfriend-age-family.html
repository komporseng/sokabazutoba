Barney Harris On The Wheel Of Time

Barney plays the role of Mat Cauthon in The Wheel of Time. One of the main characters in the show, Mat is Ta’veren and a childhood companion of Perrin Aybara and Rand al’Thor. Furthermore, Mat Cauthon is Captain-General of the Band of the Red Hand; later consort of Tuon/Fortuona, with the title of Prince of the Ravens. A former hornblower of the Horn of Valere, Barney’s character is known to the Aelfinn and Eelfinn as ‘Son of Battles’ and to the Heroes of the Horn as ‘The Gambler.’

Fans of the show have loved Barney’s portrayal of Mat Cauthon. He has been applauded for his performance as Mat Cauthon by critics as well.

Unfortunately, Barney Harris will be replaced by actor Donal Finn in Season 2 of The Wheel of Time. The reasons for the recasting of Mat Cauthon’s role are still unclear.

For what it’s worth, Barney’s name was not announced for The Wheel of Time panel. Neither did he attend the event.

Although it would be too soon to speculate the reason why Barney will not reprise his role as Mat Cauthon in The Wheel of Time, we suspect it’s personal more than professional.

Barney’s first acting credit came in the 2015 romantic comedy movie called All Roads Lead to Rome. He played the role of Tyler in that movie. The next year, Barney was a part of three projects. First off, he played Ned in the TV show The Hollow Crown. Secondly, he was in Billy Lynn’s Long Halftime Walk as Sykes, and thirdly, he played Fal in Sweet Maddie Stone.

In 2018, Barney appeared in six episodes of the series called Clique. Before appearing in The Wheel of Time, Barney was part of four other projects, which include A Brixton Tale, Brighton Beach, It’s Me, and Starboy.
