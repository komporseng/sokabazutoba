Career

He began his wrestling career in 2006 when he joined The Colony under the ring name Fire Ant. The stable gained popularity in 2007 as a result of its unique matches. The stable had a disagreement with Rudo BLK OUT, which they won. In 2008, The Colony and The UnStable developed a rivalry. Although they did not win the titles, the Colony won the Tag World Grand Prix.

After some changes in The Colony, the stable worked their way into the tag team division and won a championship opportunity. In September, the Colony defeated Osirian Portal in the Campeonatos de Parejas. Furthermore, in the Campeonatos de Parejas in 2010, The Colony was defeated by Ares and Claudio of the BDK. In the finals of the King of Trios event in 2011, The Colony defeated F.I.S.T.

In 2012, The Colony and The Swarm developed a feud. The Colony was unable to win the King of Trios competition due to The Swarm. After the introduction of new colony members with the swarm known as The Colony: Xtreme Force, The Colony, which consisted of the old members, feuded with Xtreme Force.

The plot continued even after Chikara’s absence, and all of the other members joined The Colony. In 2014, The Flood defeated The Colony: Xtreme Force, but The Colony lost. In 2018, Fire Ant and two new ants won the King of Trios event.

In 2019, Orange Cassidy made his Double or Nothing debut. In August, he joined the company and reconnected with The Best Friends. At AEW Revolution, he was defeated by PAC. Furthermore, in 2020, he began a feud with Chris Jericho. During his feud with Jericho, he rose to prominence and gained widespread popularity. He put an end to the rivalry when he defeated Jericho in a Mimosa Mayhem at All Out.

He was given two chances to win the TNT Championship but failed both times. In 2021, Kenny Omega defeated Orange Cassidy and PAC to win the AEW World Heavyweight Championship at Double or Nothing. In 2022, he and The Best Friends began a feud with The Elite and Britt Baker. He also defeated Adam Cole in a lights-out match at Beach Break. He competed in and lost the Face of the Revolution ladder match at Revolution.
